Privacy Policy for Custom Sight Words

Last updated: December 11, 2025

Custom Sight Words does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app is designed for offline use and does not require an account, login, or internet connection.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information
  • No usage data
  • No analytics
  • No tracking technologies
  • No location or device data

Children’s Privacy

This app is safe for children and collects no information from anyone, including children under 13.

Third-Party Services

The app uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or external APIs. All features operate entirely on the device.

User-Generated Content

Users may add custom sight-word lists and words. This content is stored only on the device and is never transmitted by the app.
